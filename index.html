a cozy corner

Snail started with a stubborn idea: that good coffee is worth waiting for, and waiting is worth doing on a soft chair next to a window. We're a small neighborhood spot in the heart of West Central, slinging single-origin pour-overs, espresso, and donuts baked the same morning.

No app upsells. No twelve-step ordering. Just a little menu, a slow rhythm, and a snail that thinks you're doing great.
